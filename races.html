The 2026 Season

Two races, a big journey ahead.

Race 1 · 25 July 2026

Sydney to Gold Coast

384 nm ~2–3 days Double-handed

Sydney Harbour to Queensland, tracking up the NSW coast. The East Australian Current - three knots of southerly flow - is the strategic puzzle: stay inshore to dodge it and cop the headlands, or go wide for better breeze and add miles. Our first extended offshore of the season.

Race 2 · 31 July 2026

Gold Coast to Mackay

520 nm ~3.5 days Double-handed

Six days after Gold Coast, we head further north - past Fraser Island and into tropical Queensland. Longer, hotter, and run in trade-wind conditions. Back-to-back with the first race, this is the proving ground for the whole campaign.
