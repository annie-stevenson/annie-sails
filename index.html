The mission

Why a campaign, why now.

There are two worlds I move in — engineering and sailing. This campaign is mostly about the offshore racing. But it's also about going to start lines and meeting rooms where you weren't necessarily expected, and reminding yourself that you belong there just as much as anyone else.

Sailing is a wonderful, demanding sport that benefits from a variety of mindsets and voices. So is engineering. Accessible education is how you build a sport - it lowers the barrier, introduces new people, and makes sure talent isn't gated by who you know or where you grew up. The campaign is my small contribution to that - and the website is the educational version, so the next person can have a slightly easier time finding the door.

Annie Stevenson
