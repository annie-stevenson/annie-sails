The mission

Why a campaign, why now.

Sailing is a wonderful, demanding sport that benefits from a variety of mindsets and voices. Not unlike engineering. Accessible education is how you build something great - it lowers the barrier, introduces new people, and makes sure talent isn't gated by who you know or your background.

The campaign is my small contribution to that, so the next person can have a slightly easier time finding their way.
