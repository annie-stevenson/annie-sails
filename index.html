Why this site exists

A learning hub, not a highlight reel.

Sailing has a high jargon wall. Most explanations either talk down to you or assume you already know what a kicker does. I'd rather just explain things the way I wish someone had explained them to me — clearly, with diagrams, with the trade-offs spelled out.

Every article on this site started life as a 60-second Instagram video. The site is where I get to actually finish the thought.
