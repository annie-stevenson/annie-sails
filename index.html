The mission

Why a campaign, why now.

Sailing is a wonderful, demanding sport that benefits from a variety of mindsets and voices. Not unlike engineering. I believe accessible education is how you build something great - it lowers the barrier, introduces new people, and tries to give everyone gets a fair shot.
